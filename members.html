Team
Hi, we are team iGEM ULaval of Laval University in Quebec, Canada. Our multidisciplinary team has been part of the iGEM journey since 2018. Graduate and undergraduate students from biochemistry, bioinformatics, biomedical sciences, chemistry, communication, food sciences, microbiology and software engineering have been working hard on the project aSAP through-out the two last years. Among our dynamic team, the team also includes three outstanding teachers and mentors who guide and advise us, in addition to the various experts and former ULaval iGEMers that helped us in many ways to make aSAP a reality. We are eager to show you what we have realized with aSAP this year.
